About Us

Our History

Founded in 1950, Govt. College of Teacher Education Khandwa has a proud history of fostering educational excellence in Madhya Pradesh. Over the decades, we have contributed significantly to the academic landscape, training educators who go on to inspire and lead.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate an environment where future teachers can develop the skills, knowledge, and dedication needed to create meaningful educational experiences. We aim to empower our students with a passion for lifelong learning and a commitment to social responsibility.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every educator is a guiding light for young minds. Through research, innovation, and community engagement, we strive to set new standards in teacher education.
